Guest / Website Visitor

Guest
1
Browse Public Website
Access all public pages including About ISRS, Conferences, Publications, and News without logging in.
2
Learn About ISRS
Read about the society's mission, principles, history, and current initiatives for coral reef conservation.
3
View Conference Information
See upcoming conference dates, locations, and general information. Detailed schedules may require registration.
4
Join or Register
Click "Become a Member" to join ISRS or "Register" to sign up for a conference.

ISRS Member

Member
1
Join ISRS
Complete the membership application form and pay annual dues. Receive confirmation email with member credentials.
2
Access Member Portal
Login via magic link authentication. View personalized dashboard with membership status and benefits.
3
Manage Profile
Update contact information, research interests, institutional affiliation, and communication preferences.
4
Access Member Benefits
Use member directory, access publications, receive discounted conference rates, and participate in elections.

Member Capabilities

Member directory access
Profile management
Conference discounts
Publication access
Voting rights
Networking tools

Conference Attendee

Attendee
1
Register for Conference
Select registration type (Early Bird, Standard, Student). ISRS members receive discounted rates.

Pay via credit card, PayPal, or bank transfer. Receive confirmation email with registration details.

4
Attend Conference
Check in at registration desk. Access conference app for schedules, maps, and networking.

Conference Presenter

Presenter Attendee
1
Submit Abstract
Submit abstract during call for papers. Select presentation type: oral, poster, or symposium.
2
Abstract Review
Abstract reviewed by scientific committee. Receive acceptance notification with session assignment.
3
Register & Pay
Complete conference registration (required for all presenters). Upload final abstract and presentation files.
4
Present at Conference
Deliver presentation at assigned time slot. Access speaker-ready room for AV check.

File upload portal
Speaker-ready room access
Session scheduling
Presentation guidelines

Authentication (All Users)

All Roles
1
Navigate to Login
Go to /admin/ for admin portal or click "Member Portal" link on the main website.
2
Enter Email Address
Enter your registered email address. The system uses passwordless authentication via magic links.
3
Check Email
You'll receive an email with a secure magic link (expires in 15 minutes). Click the link to authenticate.
4
Access Granted
Based on your role(s), you'll see the appropriate portal with features matching your permissions.

Security Features

Passwordless authentication
Magic link expiration (15 min)
Session management
Role-based access control
Multi-role support
Secure token storage
